Here, I'll share the core aspects of my personality that shape my journey, the inspirations that motivate me, and how my passions influence the way I approach challenges. Dive in to learn more about what drives me, my unique perspectives, and the values that guide my path forward.

Strongest Trait: Resilience

Resilience is the ability to bounce back from challenges, and it has been crucial to my academic journey. When faced with difficult assignments or personal setbacks, my resilience strengthens my ability to meet deadlines, overcome obstacles, and stay focused on my long-term goals.

Emotion: Compassion

Compassion has shaped my interactions with classmates, especially when helping others who are struggling with a topic I understand well. I enjoy offering support and seeing others succeed.

Hobby: Gaming

Gaming has been a passion of mine for years, inspiring me to pursue a career in computing. But my love may be too much at times. I sometimes get lost in the virtual worlds, spending hours. While this passion fuels my creativity and problem-solving skills, balancing it with school life can be challenging. Still, it's a journey I'm excited to navigate.

My Hero, Paz Ortega

Paz Ortega Andrade is a significant character in the Metal Gear Solid series, particularly in Peace Walker and Ground Zeroes. On the surface, she appears to be a young woman with idealistic views about world peace, even her name, "Paz" means "peace" in Spanish. She initially works alongside "Big Boss" and his group to pursue peace, but it is later revealed that she is an undercover agent with a more complex agenda, symbolizing the tangled web of deception and idealism in the quest for peace.

"In school, I also learned that peace is an unnatural state for human society. So to achieve peace, we have to create it ourselves. Crying about it won't bring it about, or make it last." Paz Ortega

This is my favorite quote from her, it highlights her perspective on the harsh reality of peace. That is something that doesn't exists naturally in human society, but something that must be fought for, created, and maintained through action. The idea that crying or simply wishing for peace won't make it a reality reflects her pragmatism and understanding of the world's complexities.

Paz is someone I look up to because of this duality between her idealism and practicality. While she longs for peace, she understands that achieving it requires struggle and effort, which is a powerful reminder that nothing meaningful comes without hard work. Her character teaches us that peace isn't something we can passively wait for, but something we have to actively pursue—even if it means facing tough, morally complex decisions. Her journey and personal sacrifices make her an intriguing figure, and despite her flaws, her ideals about peace and perseverance resonate deeply.

Personality Test

According to this test, I'm identified as a "guardian"—someone who takes pride in being dependable, helpful, and hardworking. Guardians are described as loyal partners, responsible parents, and stabilizing leaders. While I can see some truth in these traits, there's one part that doesn't quite resonate with me: being a leader, especially a responsible one. That kind of pressure feels overwhelming. However, when I reflect on the qualities associated with guardians, I do feel that they capture a lot of who I am.
