Here the Storyboard of my website

The wireframe layout for the website

The Personality and Personal Development Page follows a simple design that has sections with information, a navigation bar and a Footer.

List of webpages I created

  • Index Page: Introduces the viewer to the website and provides a starting point for navigation. Includes a picture of you that links to an email client and an overview of the website.
  • Professional Page: Showcases your resume, career vision, mission, and future career goals. It helps the viewer understand your professional aspirations and skills.
  • Personality Page: Provides insight into your personality, hobbies, and influences. The page includes a personality test, reflections, and a favorite quote or motto from a hero or mentor.Personality Page
  • Personal Development Page: Reflects on the lessons learned from Twelve Pillars and how they relate to your life. Also includes details about your academic journey and acquired skills.
  • Storyboard Page: Contains the layout design, feature list, and theme of the site. Helps explain the structure and aesthetic direction of the website.
  • Reference Page: Lists all sources, research materials, and references in APA format, ensuring academic integrity.Reference Page

Features I added to the website

  • Navigation Bar: Available on all pages for seamless navigation across the website.
  • Internal Bookmark Link: At least one internal link (bookmark) to enhance the navigation experience within a page.
  • Personality Test: Embedded screenshot of the personality test results along with a reflective analysis.
  • Free Hosting: The website will be hosted on a free platform and a link will be provided here.
